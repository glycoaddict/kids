Joseph 1 Recap

  1. told their father that they were doing a bad job (a bad report)
  2. their father loved Joseph more than his brothers (favouritism)
  3. Joseph dreamt that one day he would rule over his brothers, who would bow to him

The brothers wanted to kill Joseph, hide his body, and pretend that it was a ferocious animal that ate Joseph.

  1. They trapped him in an underground well (a "cistern")
  2. sold Joseph as a slave to Ishmaelites who took him to Egypt
  3. Lied to their father that Joseph had been killed by a wild beast.
